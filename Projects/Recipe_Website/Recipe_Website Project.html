Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, ghee, and a pinch of cardamom.
  2. Add water gradually to form a soft dough.
  3. Make small balls from the dough and deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Dip the fried balls in sugar syrup and let them soak.

Soft Barfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Khoa
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan.
  2. Add khoa and sugar, stir well.
  3. Add cardamom for flavor.
  4. Pour the mixture into a tray, let it cool, and cut into barfi shapes.

Indian Rasmlai

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Milk
  • Pistachios

Recipe

  1. Boil milk in a pan.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom to the boiling milk.
  3. Stir continuously until the milk thickens.
  4. Garnish with pistachios and serve chilled.
